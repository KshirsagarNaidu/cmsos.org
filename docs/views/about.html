About Us

We are a group of people who own plots at Cascading Meadows (CM) Phases 1 and 2 at Margondanahalli Village, Bidarahalli-2 Hobli, Bangalore East Taluk, Bangalore who are volunteering our time and effort to bring progress, change, and an alternative option by contributing to the development of the area applying our collective experience, education, wisdom, and respect, keeping the interests of our members paramount.

The highlight of the new association will be the nominal introductory membership fee of Rs.1,000/- and yearly maintenance fee based on actual expenses. We already have designated volunteers who will be visiting our plots periodically to ensure that there is no encroachment or unlawful activity within our plots, besides periodically checking the records available at the Bhoomi Website for any suspicious transactions, encumbrance, mortgage or illegal sale of our plots.

None of the founders of the association stands to gain anything personally or financially from forming this alternate association and questioning the status quo at CM, except the gratification of doing what is morally right. We aim to live by our core values such as democracy, accountability and conscientiousness.

We request each of you who own plots at CM to review the website of our association, its objectives and bye-laws. Please consider joining our association, if you share similar views about the stated objectives and the passion to protect and nurture our investments/homes in a cost conscious manner. Welcome aboard the CMSOS mission and we hope that you will decide to join this voluntary endeavor.
